The hidden maintenance chore

Air filters are easy to ignore until they create bigger problems.

Most homes rely on clean filters for steady airflow, but replacement dates and filter sizes are rarely top of mind.

Forgotten replacements

Filters often stay in place for months longer than intended because there is no simple system.

Reduced airflow

A dirty filter makes it harder for air to move through the system and through the home.

Higher energy costs

Restricted airflow can make HVAC equipment work harder, which may increase monthly energy use.

Wrong filter sizes

Many homeowners do not know the exact filter size until they pull the old one out.

FAQ

Questions before you reserve early access?

How do I know what filter size I need?

Most filters have dimensions printed on the cardboard frame. If you cannot find the size, you can upload a photo and we'll use it to help identify the right replacement.

Can I upload a photo of my current filter?

Yes. The early access form includes an optional image upload field so you can test the photo flow locally.

When will the first batch ship?

FilterFlow is currently validating demand. We'll contact early access members before the first limited batch opens.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. The planned service is designed around flexible household needs, including the ability to pause or cancel before future shipments.

Will pricing change?

Early access pricing may change because final cost depends on filter size, quantity, shipping, and supplier availability.

Do you support multiple filters?

Yes. The Home Plan is intended for homes with more than one filter, and the form asks how many filters your home uses.
