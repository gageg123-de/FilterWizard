FAQ

Questions before you reserve early access?

How do I know what filter size I need?

Most filters have dimensions printed on the cardboard frame. If you cannot find the size, you can upload a photo and we'll use it to help identify the right replacement.

Can I upload a photo of my current filter?

Yes. Filter Wizard supports photo uploads so you can share the label or frame of your current filter when you reserve early access.

Is this a subscription?

Filter Wizard is validating automatic replacement deliveries. Early access helps confirm which sizes, quantities, and delivery schedules homeowners want most.

When will the first batch ship?

Filter Wizard is currently validating demand. We'll contact early access members before the first limited batch opens.

Can I cancel or pause?

Yes. The planned service is designed around flexible household needs, including the ability to pause or cancel before future shipments.

Do you support homes with multiple filters?

Yes. The Whole Home plan is intended for homes with more than one filter, and the form asks how many filters your home uses.
